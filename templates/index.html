About Moodie

Moodie is an AI-powered tool that analyzes movie reviews to determine their sentiment. Using advanced machine learning techniques, Moodie can tell you whether a review is positive, negative, or neutral. It's the perfect companion for movie enthusiasts who want to quickly gauge public opinion on films.
